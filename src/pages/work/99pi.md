# A New Home for a Meteoric Podcast

After their first kickstarter raised $170k the podcast / radio show [99% Invisible](http://99percentinvisible.org/) still had an aging tumblr hosting their content. The show's audience was growing rapidly and they new they needed something better for hosting their content. Together we determined the best solution for hosting their content, provided them with URL shortening, and discussed how these would best fit into their social media sharing strategy. We also found them a scalable hosting solution that would allow them to continue to grow their traffic. The staff have gone on to help found Radiotopia, with 99% Invisible as a member show. This hugely successful podcast group touts 8.5 million downloads per month.

## A Media Player that Plays With You

The show itself is delivered via audio files. But the website hosts supplemental information for each podcast and there is a backlog of episodes to browse.
